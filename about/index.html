I'm an 18 year old .NET, student developer that grew up in the Chicago suburbs. In seventh grade, my friend taught me how to make subtle changes in the text of the game, Halo 4. At the time, simply changing a few strings here and there completely blew my mind. Since then, I have come to understand the ins and outs of many computer science concepts that have allowed me to create a large amount of different applications, games, and mods. This website houses all of my creations and will hopefully give you some insight into how I became the programmer I am today.
