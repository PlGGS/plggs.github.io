Animal vs Plant Proteins

Plant proteins have no saturated fats. They also have more dietary fiber, vitamin C and E, and calcium.

Animal proteins have more amino acids than their competitor, but don't exceed in much else.

Fat Myth: Cholesterol Rich Foods Are Bad For You

Many people think that cholesterol rich foods are in fact terrible for you, but the fact is, the amount
of protein in these foods more often than not doubles the amount of fat. People have begun thinking
these types of foods are bed because of their high amount cholesterol, yet new studies show that these
foods don't raise cholesterol in the blood or contribute in any way to heart disease.

Water VS Fat Soluble Vitamins

While we do store fat soluble vitamins, our bodies don't store water soluble vitamins.
This means a constant supply of water soluble vitamins are needed to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

    LDL (Low-density Lipoprotein):

    LDL is considered bad cholestrol. Risk of atherosclerosis is likely to increase. High amounts of LDL
    can cause a blood clot which can lead to a heart attack.

    HDL (High-density Lipoptrotein):

    HDL is considered good cholestrol. HDL will remove LDL from the arteries and lower your chance
    of heart disease and stroke.

    Obesity, Overweight, and Underweight:

    Obesity:

    A bodily condition in which fat builds up. This can cause low self esteem as well as depression.

    Overweight:

    A case in which someone weighs more than what is expected and or considered normal. This may
    also, like obesity, eventually lead to low self esteem as well as depression.

    Underweight:

    When a person is considered to be below the average weight that is accepted at that time. This may
    come as a surprise to some, but being underweight can also lead to the same effect of being obese or
    simply overweight.
