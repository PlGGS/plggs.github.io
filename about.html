Blake Boris (PlGGS)

I'm a 17 year old .NET, student developer. I grew up in the Chicago suburbs and discovered programming when I was in seventh grade. My friend taught me how to make subtle changes in the text of the game, Halo 4. At the time, simply changing a few strings here or there completely blew my mind, because before then, I had never understood anything about how computer programs, let alone Xbox 360 games, worked. Since then, I have come to understand the ins and outs of many computer concepts that have allowed me to create so many different applications, games, and mods, and I can't begin to explain how much fun it's been.
