Olah 2 is Olah is the first real game that I ever created. It was also the first game in our Computer Science class to make use of controllers of any kind. After asking our teacher for an extra night to work on the project to figure out the few remaining bugs, we gave a live demo of our game for not only the teacher to play, but for four of our classmates to play using wireless Xbox 360 controller. The reception was incredible. Since we were the last group to present, our class spent the rest of the period having different groups of students test out the game without a single crash! This was definitely the moment in which I decided I wanted to pursue Computer Science as a career.
