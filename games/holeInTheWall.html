Hole in the Wall is a game my friend and I wrote in VB.NET that somehow put the Xbox 360 Kinect accessory to good use. After creating the first game in the class to make use of controllers, we thought that the Kinect would be an amazing way to top what even that game had to offer. With five levels of increasing difficulty with a good amount of replayability, Hole in the Wall's easy to learn, hard to master gameplay was a hit within our Computer Science class!
